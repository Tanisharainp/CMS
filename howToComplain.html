HOW  TO  COMPLAIN

REGISTER                 LOGIN                VERIFIED           SEND COMPLAINT 




            (i) Register your Account
            (ii) Login with email and password
            (iii) Your profile will be verified
            (iv) Send your complaint


We aim to provide an excellent and consistent service to all our customers through our customer promise. If you are not atisfied with any aspect of our service,let us know so we can resolve the matter as quickly as possible.



Register


You first need to register your account by entering the details.You will then recieve a notification if your account has been registered.If you dont recieve any notificatio,please repeat the registration process again.


Login


Once your account has been registered and you have recived the notification, Login to your account by entering your email id and password.


Verification


Once you hit the submit button for login, your details will then be checked and verified.If either your email id or password doesn't match, you will be asked to login again.


Send Complaints


Once you have been verified with correct credentials, you will be taken to your profile page where you can update your profile for further needs and also complain if you have any.
